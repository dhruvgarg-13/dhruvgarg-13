ABOUT DELICIOUS TREATY

Although we are a new restaurant in Nandi Hills located in Bengaluru (JUNE 2020), we are an authentic Indian Kitchen! It's all about traditional family style cooking. All items are freshly made everyday. We even fresh grind our spices before cooking.
The food is prepared by cooks who have been in the industry for more than two decades.
